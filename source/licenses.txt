========
Licenses
========

Tasks completed by service providers (i.e. design, audio, writing) 
typically remain the property of the service provider unless ownership 
is signed over or a particular copyright or copyleft license is applied 
to it first. 

-------------
Free Licenses
-------------

Free licenses enable free usage by anyone under specified conditions. 
Copyright Permissions

Copyright permissions allow for clients to use the works under specific 
conditions.
