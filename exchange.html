Currency Exchange¶

If the client is unable to remit payment through any of the service provider’s task_payment_methods options, an escrow may be included in the transaction to facilitate exchange as a middleman between the two parties (e.g. ZEC to BTC). This would constitute an additional service with its own fees.

When using an escrow for exchange, the task_payment_methods offered by the escrow may be used instead of the service provider’s, and it will be the responsibility of the escrow agent to properly channel the payment once received from the client.
